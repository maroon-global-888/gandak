About Gandak Hospital

Established in 2010, Gandak Hospital has been serving the community of Birgunj and surrounding areas with quality healthcare services. As a private multi-specialty hospital, we are committed to providing comprehensive medical care with the highest standards of excellence.

Our team of experienced doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals work together to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients. We continuously invest in advanced medical technology and staff training to maintain our commitment to quality care.
